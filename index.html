Vision Statement

Modern blockchain interactions are limited by "how-to" complexity. The Intent Protocol Suite introduces a declarative model, allowing users to define specific outcomes. Our architecture validates, routes, and settles these intents across decentralized networks with zero-knowledge precision.

Specialized Pillar: IntentValidation.com

Intent Validation is the critical bridge between user desire and chain settlement. It ensures that solver actions match user-defined constraints. This domain serves as the primary verification oracle for the entire ecosystem, ensuring compliance and transaction integrity in real-time.
